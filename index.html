PushMailing is a personal push notification service for Android and iOS users. Users can join a business push notification service to receive relevant and effective information. More personal and intuitive than mailing campaings as each app user decides messages from which business wants to receive.

The app has a search box to enter a city or town name. Once a city is selected, the list of business and shops from the city that have joined the app will be shown. Each user will be free to join any of the business and receive push notifications from them. Imagine that you usually meet friends in the same pub every saturday and the pub has an account of PushMailing. You could join the pub and receive a push notification with information when the pub manager wants to report something to their followers, for example, "Today your favourite beer half price".

FAQ

What about Pricing?
Pushes will be sold in packs starting at 10$ for 10000 pushes and will be discounts for bigger packs.
Which are the benefits for the business and shops?
They will gain lot of customers just sending some pushes. Imagine that one pub has 10000 followers, so they just need to spend 10$ to send one push to each and attract them to the pub with the offer sent.
